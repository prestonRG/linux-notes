# Resident Evil 6

## Stability
- Add `PROTON_NO_ESYNC=1 %command%` to launch options.
- Use [GE-Proton7-49](https://github.com/GloriousEggroll/proton-ge-custom/releases/tag/GE-Proton7-49)

## Fix crashes when transitioning into/out of cutscenes.
1. Download this mod: [Resident Evil 6 - DX11 DX12 DXVK Vulkan and Uncap-FPS Support Mod](https://www.nexusmods.com/residentevil6/mods/504?tab=description)
2. Use the DXVK version for Linux and place its contents in the game's folder.
3. `Add WINEDLLOVERRIDES="d3d9=n,b" %command%` to launch options.
